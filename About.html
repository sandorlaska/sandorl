A green workplace is a good workplace

We care about the impact we have on the environment and believe that we by using environmentally friendly office cleaning products and methods we can help our customers minimise damage to the environment.

We use recyclable and biodegradable cleaning products fully biodegradable bin liners free from toxic ingredients. All products are neutral and safe on all surfaces. We thoroughly check our suppliers so that we ensure they have the same environmental values and policies. We choose all fabric we use and wear from environmentally responsible and sustainable sources.

All companies we work with share our views on the importance of having a responsible attitude towards the environment that helps keep our planet for the future generations.


An office that represents a high level of cleanliness makes it easier to work in!

Our team is comprised of professional experts

All our employees undergo thorough vetting before hire and takes part in our company training to get familiar with all the details and practices of cleaning homes and offices.

We take responsibility for the job we do

We strive to provide the highest level of service to our clients. Each and every customer is valuable for us therefore we kindly ask you to notify us in case of even the smallest degree of dissatisfaction.

We undertake one-off and regular assignments too

Our professional staff and the supervisory team behind them ensure that your business premises will be perfectly clean at all times. We offer routine cleaning, deep cleaning and tailor-de cleaning solution for offices.
